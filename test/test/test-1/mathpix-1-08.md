Example 8
Simplify $(x-2)\left(2 x^{2}-3 x+4\right)$.
Solution
$$
\begin{array}{l}
2 x^{2}-3 x+4 \\
\frac{x \quad x-2}{-4 x^{2}+6 x-8} \leadsto^{-2\left(2 x^{2}-3 x+4\right)} \\
\frac{2 x^{3}-3 x^{2}+4 x}{2 x^{3}-7 x^{2}+10 x-8} \preccurlyeq x\left(2 x^{2}-3 x+4\right) \\
\end{array}
$$
